Hot Cocoa Recipe

Ingredients

  • hot coffee
  • milk
  • optional

Steps

  1. Scoope hot cocoa mix into a mug.
  2. heat milk into the mug, stirring gently as you pour
  3. pour milk into the mug, stirring gently as you pour
  4. Stir until mix is fully dissolved in the milk
  5. top with marshmallows if you must
